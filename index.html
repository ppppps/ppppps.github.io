Yuchen Wang (王语晨)

Biography

Yuchen Wang is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). His research interests include neuromorphic computing, spiking neural networks, computational vision, and machine learning.

Education

  • 2021.09 - Present: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (Ph.D)
  • 2019.09 - 2021.06: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (M.S)
  • 2015.09 - 2019.09: Sichuan Agricultural University (B.S)

Selected Publications

  • Signed Neuron with Memory: Towards Simple, Accurate and High-Efficient ANN-SNN Conversion, IJCAI-22.
  • Supervised Learning in Multilayer Spiking Neural Networks With Spike Temporal Error Backpropagation, IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems.
  • Deep Spiking Neural Network with Neural Oscillation and Spike-Phase Information, AAAI-21.
  • A new recursive least squares-based learning algorithm for spiking neurons, Neural Networks.
  • Bio-inspired Model Based on Global-Local Hybrid Learning in Spiking Neural Network, IJCNN-21.
  • Spatio-Temporal Sequential Memory Model With Mini-Column Neural Network, Frontiers in Neuroscience.
  • Unsupervised multi-layer spiking convolutional neural network using layer-wise sparse coding, ICONIP-20.
